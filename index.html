Our Facility

Owned and Operated by Tracey Badley-Walton, Spellbound Farm is a Hunter/Jumper Training and Show Stable located in Van Alstyne, Texas, just 40 miles north of downtown Dallas.

Spellbound Farm offers Boarding, Training services and Lessons. We attend local level shows around North Texas and rated "A" shows in Texas, Oklahoma, and Kentucky.

We offer a friendly, safe and fun atmosphere for both horses and riders!
